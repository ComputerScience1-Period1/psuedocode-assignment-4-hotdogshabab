Spoons

Rules & Setup:
1. Take the jokers out of the pile.
2. Decide who will be the first person, the trash, and in the middle.
3. You need one less spoon than the total amount of players.
4. Place the spoons in the middle.
5. Each player gets 4 cards and may only have 4 cards in their hand at a time.
6. The first player takes the top card from the deck and can either pass it on the next person or replace it with a card in his hand and pass the unwanted card.
7. The next player then takes the card passed on and does the same to his hand.
8. This continues until the last person, who takes the passed on card and places it into a discard or trash pile
9. If there are still spoons remaining in the middle and the deck is finished:
   then the trash pile will be handed to the first player to be used.
10. To obtain the first spoon, a player must have 4 of a kind in their hand.
11. Once the first spoon has been taken, the remaining players may take the spoons.
12. The player who doesn't have a spoon once the round is over loses.
13. If a spoon is mistakenly taken:
   then restart the round.

Turns:
You are in the middle.
Recieve 4 cards. 
If you have four cards of the same number or someone has taken 1 spoon from the middle:
   Try to grab 1 spoon from the middle.
   If you are able to obtain the spoon: 
      You win. 
   Else:
      You lose.

If no spoons remain in the middle and you don't have a spoon in your hand:
   You lose. 

While no spoons have been taken from the middle:     //this while statement just directs you on whether or not to take the card
   Look at the card that is passed onto you.         /passed onto you
   If that card matches any one of yours:
      If you have 3 cards of the same number and the card passed onto you matches those 3 cards:
         Take the card passed onto you and pass on the non-matching card.
         Grab 1 spoon from the middle.
         If you are able to obtain the spoon: 
            You win. 
         Else:
            You lose.
      Else if you have 2 cards of the same number and the card passed onto you matches those 2 cards:
         Take that card and pass on a non-matching card.
      Else:
         Take the matching card and pass on a non-matching card.
   Else:
      Pass the card onto the next person.

Grab 1 spoon.    
If you are able to obtain the spoon:             
   You win. 
Else:
   You lose.
